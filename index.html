Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is the most populous country in Africa with over 200 million people. It is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and ethnic groups. The country is rich in natural resources, particularly oil, and has a vibrant film industry known as "Nollywood." Major cities include Lagos, the economic hub, Abuja, the capital, and Kano in the north. Nigeria is home to iconic landmarks like Zuma Rock, Yankari National Park, and the Olumo Rock.
